My Projects

sdgbc

A Nintendo Game Boy & Game Boy Color emulator featuring video & sound emulation, debugging tools, and some features not available on the real hardware (such as on-demand pausing and fast-forwarding of the emulation).

It includes support for emulating most major cartridge hardware configurations and the ability to import and export save files (automatically and manually).

Won the 2018 best final year B.Sc./M.Comp. Computer Science project prize at the University of Leicester.
