Welcome!

I'm a B.Sc. Computer Science graduate with UK 1st-class honours who has enjoyed programming since childhood. I've always been interested by the ability programming has to turn ideas into something substantial; I often describe it as the closest thing we will ever have to magic!

I began my programming journey tinkering with QBasic when I was 8 years old. Since then, I've dabbled in languages such as C#, Visual Basic, Java, Rust, Python, Lua, Haskell and others, but my preferred tools of the trade are C++ and C.
